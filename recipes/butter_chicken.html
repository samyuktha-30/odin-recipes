Butter_chicken Recipes

Description

Butter chicken, is a popular Indian dish made with tender chicken cooked in a creamy tomato sauce. It features a blend of butter, cream, and spices like garam masala, giving it a rich, mildly spiced flavor. Often served with naan or rice, itâ€™s a comforting and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken
  • Onion
  • tomato
  • Butter
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Ginger Garlic paste
  • Spices
  • Heavy cream
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger, garlic, lemon juice, and spices for 30 minutes to 2 hours.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken in a pan until browned and cooked through.
  3. Saute onions in butter until golden,then add ginger and garlic paste.
  4. Add purees tomatoes and cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Stir in cream,garam masala,kasuri methi and salt.
  6. Combine cooked chicken with the sauce and simmer until its well cooked
