Brownie Recipe

Description

Brownies are rich, chocolatey treats with a fudgy or cakey texture, made from a simple mix of butter, sugar, eggs, cocoa powder, and flour. They're baked until set and cut into squares, perfect for a sweet indulgence.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup (40g) cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder (optional for slightly fluffier texture)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking pan
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat, then remove from heat.
  3. Mix in sugar with the melted butter until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Combine dry ingredients: sift together flour, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, salt, and baking powder.
  6. Mix dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until well combined.
  7. Pour batter into the greased pan and spread evenly.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  9. Cool and cut into squares before serving. Enjoy!
